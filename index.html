About Me

In March of 2018 I fulfilled my Bachelor's degree obligations at University of Crete where I chose to specialize in Sciences, through classes such as Quantum Mechanics, Optics & Waves, Electromagnetism and Classical Mechanics. That step led me to the Physics and Astronomy Department of University of Sussex where I completed my Master's degree in Quantum Technology as being part of the Emergent Photonics (EPic) Laboratory, which is the forefront of nonlinear optical physics and Terahertz Photonics.

Graduate Teaching Assistant

Start Date: February 2020
End Date: June 2020

Aerospace Structures & Aerospace Engines

In this module we will learn the fundamentals on how aerospace structures shake and vibrate. The two main areas we will cover are: structural vibration and rigid body mechanics. In structural vibration, a single degree of freedom model will help us to study the response, and forced vibration, of systems subjected to steady state, impulse and arbitrary loading. In rigid body mechanics we will analyse two-dimensional mechanisms and the dynamics of rigid rotors, including gyroscopic motion.
